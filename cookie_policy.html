COOKIE POLICY FOR THRIIVE ART & SOUL LLP


At ThriiveArt & Soul LLP ("Thriive", "we", "us" or "our"), we believe in being clear and open about how we collect and use data related to you. In the spirit of transparency, this policy provides detailed information about how and when we use cookies. This cookie policy applies to ourwebsite, located at, www.thriive.in

By using the Website, you are consenting to our use of cookies in accordance with this cookie policy. If you do not agree to our use of cookies in this way, you should set your browser settings accordingly or not use the Website. If you disable the cookies that we use, this may impact your user experience while on the Website.

What are cookies?

Cookies are files or small pieces of information that may be stored on your computer (or other internet enabled devices, such as a smartphone or tablet) when you visit the Website. Cookies enable us to identify your device, secure your access to the Website generally, and even help us know if someone attempts to access your account from a different device. Cookies also enable you to easily share content on the Website and help us serve relevant ads to you.

Why do we use cookies?

We use cookies to provide you with the best experience possible. Cookies can be used to recognize you when you visit the Website, remember your preferences, and give you a personalized experience that's in line with your settings. We use cookies to make the Website easier to use and to better tailor the Website and Thriive's services ("Services") to your interests and needs. Cookies help us provide, protect and improve the Services. Cookies may also be used to help speed up your future activities and experience on the Website. Cookies also make your interactions with Thriive faster and more secure.

What type of cookies do we use?

We use two types: persistent cookies and session cookies. A persistent cookie helps us recognize you as an existing user, so it is easier to return to the Website or interact with our Services without signing in again. Session cookies are temporary cookies that remain on your device until you leave the Website.

Do we use third party cookies?

When you visit the Website you may receive cookies from third party websites or domains. We endeavor to identify these cookies before they are used so that you can decide whether or not you wish to accept them. Please note that the names of cookies, pixels and other technologies may change over time. More information about these cookies may be available on the relevant third party's website.

How can I control or delete cookies?

Most internet browsers are initially set up to automatically accept cookies. You can change the settings to block cookies or to alert you when cookies are being sent to your device. There are a number of ways to manage cookies. Please refer to your browser instructions or help screen to learn more about how to adjust or modify your browser settings.

If you do not want us to use cookies when you visit the Website, you can opt-out of some cookies on your settings page www.thriive.in . In order for us to recognize that you have opted out of cookies, we have to place our Thriiveopt-out cookie on your device so we can know not to set other Thriive cookies the next time you visit. If you do not want to receive cookies, you can also change your browser settings on your computer or other device you are using to access our Services. If you use Thriive without changing your browser settings, we will assume that you are happy to receive all cookies on the Website. Most browsers also provide functionality that lets you review and erase cookies, including Thriive cookies.

If you disable the cookies that we use, this may impact your experience while on the Website, for example you may not be able to visit certain areas of the Websiteor you may not receive personalized information when you visit the Website.

If you use different devices to view and access the Website (e.g. your computer, smartphone, tablet etc.) you will need to ensure that each browser on each device is adjusted to suit your cookie preferences.

To find out more about cookies, including how to see what cookies have been set and how to manage and delete them, visit www.allaboutcookies.org .
