about thriive

Thriive is a theory. A simple one - a belief that all it takes to transform the ordinary to extraordinary is a pulse of energy.

That all it takes to go from struggling “inside the box” to leaping “outside the box” is but a vibrating thought. A single instant, a moment of light, a revelation can at times breaks down those walls, and suddenly we begin to Thriive.

our story

Fifteen years ago unbelievable coincidences in Shreans & Pinky Daga’s lives led them to masters, gurus, speakers, teachers, and meditators from all over the world!

Being witness to spontaneous healings, rejuvenated lives, renewed relationships made them realize that - anything is possible! That heaven is a place on Earth.

Myriad paths to bliss, to knowing YOU! Pick your path and begin your quest back to yourself. Discover. Empower. THRIIVE.

pillars of thriive

Our pillars are the foundation that Thriive is built on. We take root in their power.

Brahmarshi Patriji

Brahmarshi Subash Patri founded the Pyramid Spiritual Societies movement in 1980, spreading the science of meditation to thousands of villages in South India. Drawing strength from the great masters that came before him he bestows knowledge and wisdom of anapanasati meditation and other paths one can take on this journey of enlightenment. But rather than being hailed as a Guru, his mission is to awaken the Inner Guru within all. He was recently awarded the Life Positive Good Karma Award by Speaking Tree.
www. pssmovement.org

Dr. Newton & Dr. Lakshmi

Dr. Newton and Dr.Lakshmi — both medical doctors - met, married, and went on to change the landscape of past life regression therapy in India. Together they’ve travelled the world with their workshops and seminars on PLR, Rebirthing, Life After Death etc. and are regarded as global experts on Past Life Regression therapy.
www.luglobal.org



Shreans Daga

The vice chairman of the Pyramid Valley International has aided hundreds of masters in spreading their message of light throughout the world. Shreans's main focus and research has led him to a discover the world of quantum energy medicine and the Science of Spirituality. Through Thriive, he dreams of making this world accessible to all souls.

team thriive

Pinky Daga

Pinky is the voice of art at Thriive. A Ph.D in literature & theatre arts from Boston University,
Pinky created Indigo Kids Edutainment a theatre production house for children's
theatre in Mumbai. She passionately believes that the soul speaks through
any form of art. A soul searcher since the last two decades, she conceptualized
Thriive Art and Soul as a platform to integrate the world of creative arts
in everyone’s soul journey.

Kaushik Daga

Executive Director, Thriive, is a tennis enthusiast. He has always felt drawn
to his roots at Betul, Madhya Pradesh, where, hugely inspired by his
brother/mentor, Shreans, he set up the Pyramid Wellness Centre and the
state-of- the art Satpuda Valley High School. His background in agriculture
has empowered him to develop organic Soyabean seeds, benefitting a 2000+ farmer network.
He finds his calling in the community development work he has undertaken at Betul, and believes that Higher Powers have propelled him to walk down the spiritual path, which is now an organized effort in the form of Thriive.in
